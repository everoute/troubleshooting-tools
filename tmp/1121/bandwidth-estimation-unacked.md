## Analysis Notes

### Formula
```
Estimated_BW = unacked × MSS × 8 / RTT
             = unacked × 1448 × 8 / (RTT_ms / 1000)
```

### Why use unacked instead of cwnd?

- `cwnd`: Theoretical maximum allowed in-flight packets
- `unacked`: Actual in-flight packets at the moment of sampling
- `unacked × MSS / RTT` reflects the **actual sending rate**
- `cwnd × MSS / RTT` is just the **upper bound**

### Key Observations

1. **High variance in estimates**: Due to RTT and unacked fluctuations
2. **Average may exceed delivery_rate**: Because of sampling at burst moments
3. **Median is more stable**: Less affected by outliers
4. **cwnd utilization is low**: Only ~25-30% of cwnd is typically used
